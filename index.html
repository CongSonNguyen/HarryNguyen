HoloStudy

Since the holographic technology has been introduced in 1971, Very few apps and practices have been done so far. As i understand the huge potential of this futuristic technology in many fields especially education, the ‘holostudy’ was born. The project will focus on developing apps for the hardware ‘hololens’, and then equip it to all classes. Holostudy will help student to interact with holograms within a small environment such as labs, lecture halls. There will be no more paradigm education but 3-D graphic and animation instead which will enhance teaching quality and student engagement during class time.

It is absolutely true that the future of the world is in my classroom today and that is the reason why I want to make holostudy reality. Holograms can bring tons of opportunities for students. For instance, when students learn about human biology, instead of viewing it on 2-D screen, holograms can bring the entire human cardiovascular system to real life and so students can walk around and explore them in detail. Biological study is just one of many fields that can be applied holograms. When it comes to concepts that are too small, too complex, or too abstract that students cannot understand by reading, holograms can visualize it and make it easy to understand. Holostudy is the future learning style.

The hololens now enable teachers to bring any concepts to mixed reality (MR). Mix reality a.k.a MR is the world where digital world combined with the real world. When students wear hololens, they will see the same 3D holograms as what their teacher seeing . This feature create a lot of opportunities for interaction and collaboration between students and teachers and It makes learning more interesting and attractive therefore more effective. However, there will be a set of apps that need to be developed in order to prepare holographic lessons.

HoloStudy contains three components

  • Hololens (Hardware): Hololens is a headset that enables users to see and interact with holograms within the small to medium area such as classes, labs. No wires, no phone required, hololens has its own built-in computer, so you can walk around freely.
  • Operating System: Considered hololens is a computer, it needs an operating system like other computers. The window interface will be designed for this hololens, so users can interact with the system like they interact with normal computers.
  • Apps: There will be apps for hololens just like app for iPhone or Android

If the holostudy project is successfully put into practice, we will have the entirely new teaching and learning method that we can go beyond the screen. Teachers will interact with students through holograms, a complete new way to visualize studies which can not be described by writing. With holostudy, students will have a better understanding of twhat they are learning and also their interest in learning will be greatly enhanced
